Welcome

I’m Wikus, This is my website. I was born and raised in Stellenbosch (close to Cape Town in South Africa), and went on to study at Stellenbosch University. I completed my Masters degree in the field of robotics and computer vision. After completing my studies I started working as one of the first employees at Praelexis, a machine learning startup. Building a company from the ground up has been an interesting journey. Rapid innovation coupled and creating production ready software has been our competitive advantage.

After spending my entire life up to now in Stellenbosch I decided that it's time to move on, and that that move should be to Australia. My skilled independent visa was granted early in November 2018, allowing me to live and work anywhere in Australia. I'm currently looking for a position in Australia and would welcome any opportunity involving the development of machine learning software and solutions. I find pleasure in taking on new challenges and ultimately becoming better at whatever it is I do. With that in mind, my current focus include deep learning and the surrounding fields of machine learning and AI. I would like to grow my skills in an environment that values research and surround myself with people from whom I can learn.
